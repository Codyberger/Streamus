Streamus turns YouTube into the music player you always wanted. Built as a Google Chrome extension, Streamus is the perfect twist to a free service you already know and love.

Install now

Stop bookmarking YouTube

Add videos to Streamus and forget the bookmarks! Your collections will still tag along between PCs.

Discover new music

Streamus helps you discover new music. Radio Mode automatically provides new music by learning from your playlists!

One less tab

Ever accidentally close the song you were listening to? Yeah, me too. Streamus runs behind the scenes so you can listen to your music without feeling crowded by a ton of tabs.

Share everything

No limitations. Share videos and playlists with just a click, just like in Spotify, with the share.streamus.com URL!
